Frequent Asked Questions

Some of the most frequent asked questions

Is there advertisement in your app?
No. We do not have advertisement in our application as we know how annoying advertisement can be.

How do I report a bug if i had encountered them?
If you found a bug in our application. You can send a report to us through our application. Under Account, You can select help centre and press the "Other issues? Contact us to let us know" button to send a report to us. You can also send us an email to let us know about the bug.

Why should I download your application instead of other transportation application?
Our Application has unique features that other transportation application do not have and this allow our application stand out from other transportation Application. You can find out more about our application under Our Product.

When is the official release date of your application?
Our app will be releasing soon. We dont have an official date for release yet. Follow our social media to stay tuned and find out when we will be releasing our application

Will you be updating your demo application in the mean time?
Yes. We will try to fix all the issues we can every update to ensure that when our application officially released, our application will run smoothly and not bugs. Your feedback and suggestion is important for you. Email us or Contact us to let us know any improvement you want to see. Thank you

Do I have to pay to download your application when it officially out?
No, you do not have to pay for our application.Our application will be free. We believed that essential application should not be paid as this is an application that will help with your daily life.

If you still have question or you did not find the answer you are looking for, You can contact us or send us a email.

Our PRODUCT

WeTransport Application by WeTransport Company

Here, you can see our prototype app. It is a beta version of our official app. You can go ahead and give it a shot. We will continue to update our application to make it run more smoothly or new features. You can also take a look at our short video about our application. The video summary up our application features. If you have any feedback or suggestion, you can contact us or email us to let us know about it and we will try to implement your suggestion. Thank you!

Features of our Application

Transportation Feed
This feed will provide the latest news for Transportation. News like any accident, incident or any article on newspaper. This will help to ensure that the user will know all the latest new for Transportation. This feed will also provide our latest news. We will alert the user about every update we do and also provide what we have changed. The latest reward for the month will be announced through here too so look out for those reward!

Breakdown Feed
This feed will provide all the breakdown that have happened recently or today for MRT. This is to ensure that our user will know about the latest breakdown and they can make changes for their mode of Transportation before they start their journey or during their journey. This can help user to save some time and we hoped that user will find this feature to be helpful as it can come in handy!

Find Me A Route
This feature is part of Breakdown Feed. In the event of a breakdown, when you do not know what bus to take to reach your destination, this feature will help you with that. Just simply enter the location you are in and enter your destination location. We will try our best to provide you the best route you should take to ensure that our user will reach their destination in a short amount of time. We hope user will find this feature to be helpful.

Bus location arrival
This is a cool feature we are trying to do that most transportation application do not have. Instead of showing the arrival time of a bus, we will show you the bus location using your GPS. This will allow the user to see where is the bus currently and give an estimated time for the bus to arrive to your current bus stop. We implemented this function is to provide the user a better accuracy for bus arrival as we know that sometimes transportation application will not provide the most accurate arrival time which caused user to feel frustrated with the application. We hope that user will be satisfied with this feature.

Ez-link Top-up
Yup. You saw that! You will no longer need to queue up at the top up machine to top up your ez-link card. Simply key in your ez-link card information and you can top up your ez-link card through our application. Sound convenient right? This will help to save your time! Plus, you can keep track of your balance. This will allow you to know when you need to top up your ez-link card. Your ez-link card will no longer run out of value anymore. And there are reward for topping up your ez-link! Sound great right?

Reward Page
All of us love rewards right? We will like to reward our user for using our application. Simply top up your ez-link card through our application and you can get some rewards. Rewards include like discounted price for your next top up, promo code for Grab, Kopitam, Golden Village and many more. This is the way we can show appreciation for our user that uses our application and long time user will get more rewards! We hope you will like the rewards we are giving to you!
